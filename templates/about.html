About the University

Alagappa University, situated in Karaikudi, Tamil Nadu, was established in 1985 and is recognized for its commitment to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement. The university is accredited with an A+ Grade by NAAC and is a hub of academic excellence in South India.

The university offers a wide range of programs through its constituent colleges, affiliated institutions, and distance education mode. It fosters an environment of innovation, inclusivity, and student-centered learning, equipping learners with knowledge and skills to thrive globally.
